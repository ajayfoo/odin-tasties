Tasties' Dal Recipe

Description

In Indian cuisine, dal, are dried, split pulses (e.g., lentils, peas, and beans) that do not require soaking before cooking. India is the largest producer of pulses in the world. The term is also used for various soups prepared from these pulses. These pulses are among the most important staple foods in South Asian countries, and form an important part of the cuisines of the Indian subcontinent.

Ingredients

  • Pigeon pea
  • Chana dal
  • Yellow split eas
  • Split mung beans
  • Urad dal
  • Masoor dal
  • Rajma dal
  • Mussyan
  • Panchratna dal
  • Moth Bean
  • Turmeric
  • Salt
  • Tomatoes
  • Kokum
  • Unripe Mango
  • Jaggery
  • Chaunk
  • Tadka
  • Fodni
  • Cumin Seeds

Steps

  1. Boil dal in water
  2. Add Turmeric and Salt
  3. Add Fried Garnish
  4. Add Tomatoes and Kokum
  5. Mix it well
  6. Let it boil for 10 minutes and serve
